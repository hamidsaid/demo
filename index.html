WELCOME TO UNIHUB

MAKING LEARNING EASIER

The first ever platform dedicated to help you to get the most out of your field of study by organizing your timetable, study materials and announcements from DARUSO and your class representatives.

EXPLORE

SERVICES

timetable

The Unihub platform provides students with a schedule to ensure that each class has particular learning hours of a particular period to reduce confusion while learning. Students are made clear about the subject they have to study in a particular period,without having to search for their timetable every time through the website.

course resources

Students can simply access various learning resources such as reference books, pastpapers,lecture notes and everything of that sort. This simplifies the trouble of downloading books from websites.

announcements

Students are given alert on various events happening in the university about university examinations,meetings, graduations,tests and any new announcements that concern the university and the students need to know about.
